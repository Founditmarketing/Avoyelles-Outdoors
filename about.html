Our Story

Deeply Rooted in Avoyelles Parish

Avoyelles Outdoors Inc. was founded with a single mission: to provide the Marksville community with high-quality outdoor equipment and supplies that you can't find at big-box chains. As a locally owned and operated business, we understand the specific needs of Louisiana property owners — from managing humid summer lawn care to maintaining pristine pools in the heat.

We don't just sell products; we provide solutions. Whether you're a homeowner needing plumbing parts for a weekend repair or a landscaper looking for the right power equipment, our staff treats every customer like a neighbor. Our team has years of expertise in small engine repair, chemical balancing for pools, and general hardware expertise.

When you shop with us, you're supporting a local business that gives back to its community. We pride ourselves on having the "hard to find" parts and the knowledge to help you get the job done right the first time.
